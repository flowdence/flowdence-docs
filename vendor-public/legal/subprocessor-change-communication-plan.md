# Subprocessor Change Communication Plan

Last updated: 2026-02-16
Owner: Flowdence Security and Legal
Applies to app: All Flowdence Marketplace cloud apps
Review cadence: Quarterly and whenever a subprocessor is added or replaced

## Purpose

Define how Flowdence notifies customers when subprocessors are added or replaced.

## Baseline commitments

- Maintain a public list of authorized subprocessors.
- Provide advance notice before material subprocessor changes.
- Provide a path for customer concerns and objections.

## Notification timeline

- Target notice window: at least 30 days before a new subprocessor starts processing customer personal data.

## Notification contents

Each notice includes:

- Subprocessor legal name
- Processing location/region
- Service purpose and data categories
- Expected effective date
- Contact channel for questions or objections

## Objection handling

- Customers may raise concerns within 5 business days of notice.
- Flowdence will evaluate concerns in good faith and discuss reasonable mitigation options.

## Publication and channels

- Public policy page in the Flowdence Confluence space
- Direct notice via support channel where required

## Operational owner

- `security@flowdence.io`
