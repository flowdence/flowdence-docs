## Purpose

Define how Flowdence adopts the Atlassian-supported Bonterms standard end-user agreement and applies app-specific Provider-Specific Terms.

## Policy

- Default legal base for cloud listings: Bonterms standard end-user agreement.
- App-level customization: Provider-Specific Terms only.
- Data processing terms: linked DPA where required.

## Why this model

- Reduces legal divergence between apps.
- Keeps customer review predictable.
- Supports app-specific risk treatment through addenda.

## Per-app implementation steps

1. Confirm app listing legal mode is Bonterms-based in Marketplace configuration.
2. Add or update app-specific Provider-Specific Terms.
3. Link app-specific addendum and DPA documents.
4. Validate support and privacy links in listing match published pages.
5. Record legal revision in app decision log.
