## Purpose

This page explains how Flowdence uses a Data Processing Addendum (DPA) for cloud app services and what customers can expect.

## Why this exists

A DPA clarifies:

- Data roles (who is Controller vs Processor)
- Security and privacy obligations
- Subprocessor governance
- International transfer safeguards
- Retention and deletion controls

## Flowdence data-role model

- Customer is generally the Data Controller for End-User Data processed through the app.
- Flowdence is generally the Data Processor for that End-User Data.
- Flowdence acts as an independent Data Controller for limited operational data required for account, billing, support, and security purposes.

## Customer benefits

- Clear accountability and legal structure.
- Better transparency around subprocessors and transfers.
- Defined handling for deletion and support requests.
- Predictable security and audit evidence expectations.
